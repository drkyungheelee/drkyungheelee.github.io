About

I am an Associate Professor at Central Michigan University. My research focuses on AI/ML-based analysis of electronic health records (EHR), cancer risk prediction, rare cancer detection, and population health. My work emphasizes early detection, screening optimization, and causal modeling for precision population health.

Research

  • AI/ML-based cancer risk prediction using EHR data
  • Rare cancer detection and early diagnosis
  • Causal modeling and counterfactual analysis
  • Population health and health disparities
